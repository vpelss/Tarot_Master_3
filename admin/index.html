You may text chat, talk over the phone or skype as desired. You may also adjust the size sliders to suite the clients screen resolution. You can also quickly adjust the card table size by clicking and dragging the lower right corner handle.
Choose a spread template underlay if desired. It will resize when you resize the card table. It can be more or less visible depending on the Card Table Opacity slider. It is easy to add your own. Just upload a template image and add a html select option to the index.html file.
